## 💡 Analysis & Recommendations

### Why English Medium Students Perform Better?

1. **Language Advantage**
   - Better communication skills
   - Easier to understand technical content
   - Better interview performance

2. **Educational Quality**
   - English medium schools often have better resources
   - More exposure to competitive exams
   - Better preparation for placements

3. **CGPA Difference**
